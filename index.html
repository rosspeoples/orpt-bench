OpenCode benchmark publication

Requests per solved task, published for fast comparison.

ORPT-Bench measures how many OpenCode requests a model spends per successful benchmark task. This page keeps the live benchmark output focused on the parts you actually compare: charts, sortable tables, scoring context, and historical snapshots.

Hint: every comparison table is sortable and starts in composite-score order.
How to read the ranking
Composite score keeps correctness dominant, then rewards efficient successful runs with ORPT, cost, and wall time.
